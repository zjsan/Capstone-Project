ABOUT US

OUR SERVICE

Our system was made for the reason of helping students and instructors who are not familiar with the different rooms of the CIS and CAB Building. We thought of developing an AI that will give the direction of where our users are going. Our AI uses a particular search algorithm that not only give the direction of the intended user's destination but also it will calculate the most optimal path for the user's destination. The generated path are stricltly bounded by the building's floor plans. Thus it does not include other paths when the users enters the building.
