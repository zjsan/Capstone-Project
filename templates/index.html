OUR SERVICE

Our system was made for the reason of helping students and instructors who are not familiar with the different rooms of the CIS and CAB Building. We thought of developing an AI that will give the direction of the user's destination. Our AI uses a particular search algorithm that not only give the direction of the intended user's destination but also calculates the most optimal path for the user's destination. The generated path are also bounded by the building's floor plans and building's structure during the duration of the develeopment of the AI.
